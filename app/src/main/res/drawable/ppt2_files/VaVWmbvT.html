Critical Limitations

Limited Functionality

The mobile web interface offers a restricted toolset. Contributors cannot access advanced editing features available on the desktop site, creating a barrier for power users.

Complex Data Management

Managing structured knowledge is cumbersome. Users struggle to add references, edit qualifiers, or handle complex statements effectively on a small touch screen.

User Constraints

The lack of a native experience leads to inefficient workflows. Zooming, scrolling, and non-optimized inputs significantly reduce productivity for volunteers on the move.
